◆ Core Selection Criteria

  • Beta below 1.10 — moves less than the overall market
  • Market cap above $100B — size provides stability
  • Positive free cash flow for 5+ consecutive years
  • Dividend history or consistent buyback program
  • Industry leadership with defensible competitive moat
  • Strong balance sheet — low debt-to-equity ratio

◆ Growth Selection Criteria

  • High total addressable market (TAM) with early penetration
  • Revenue growth rate above 20% YoY
  • Structural tailwind (AI, quantum, energy drinks, etc.)
  • High beta accepted — volatility is the price of upside
  • Smaller or mid-cap with room to 10x if thesis plays out
  • Position sizing discipline — never over-allocate to one growth bet

About This Project

I built this as a student pursuing a mathematical degree and working toward going to a four-year university. This watchlist reflects real research into risk-tiered investing — understanding the difference between capital preservation and capital growth is one of the first principles of portfolio management. Every pick here has a thesis, and every thesis is backed by fundamentals I can defend. This project is my way of doing finance, not just studying it.
